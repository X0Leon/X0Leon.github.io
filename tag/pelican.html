My new static blog was launched during this National day using Pelican and Github. With lots of trials in these two days, this website works finally. Since I am a newbie, I just take many things from other guys (Thanks!). This theme is mainly taken from Cyrille Rossant's blog, which is modified from Pure theme. Although some small changes have been made from Rossant's, yet I wanna tweak it more later.

The original reason why I made this blog is because I want to give some notes and hand out the homeworks as a TA of General Physics(B)-II course this semester. Moreover, I had a php blog before, but I didn't use it for ages and also lost the password. So Why not a new one? :)

Now, I write the blogs in Markdown, but I wanna try reStructuredText to support LaTex equations. Maybe some plugins can help me even in Markdown. Moreover, I'd like to make IPython Notebook survive in this blog. I'll see what I can do. :)

Update: So, why domuse? Actually, it's the combination of two words, Do and Muse, that is, Practice and Thinking. OK, that's the story of the website name if you are curious.
